Where Web Development is Going

Web Development has been getting more advanced each year. More students are taking coding classes, and more Websites are being created every day. More and more new AI features make coding even easier, and now can even create enitre websites for you without you even knowing how to code.

Games are becomming a big part of web developing, and they keep becoming more advanced. Graphics, speeds, and quality increase often, and with more coders, more people are creating games. Now, it isn't just big coorporations, but also regular people on their home computer developing games that get over a million people to play it.

With the growth of the internet, web development has exploded in popularity, and will only keep growing as the web becomes more user friendly.
